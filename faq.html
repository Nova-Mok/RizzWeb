Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Rizz?

Rizz is an AI-powered fashion assistant designed to enhance your online shopping experience. We offer personalized outfit recommendations, help you discover new products that match your style, and provide fashion tips from top influencers to help you achieve the looks you love.

How does Rizz work?

Using advanced AI technology, Rizz provides personalized fashion suggestions based on your preferences. Our multi-model vision technology allows you to quickly find products that match your style, while our AI offers real-time feedback to help you build cohesive outfits. Whether you're shopping for an occasion or replicating influencer trends, Rizz ensures you make confident fashion choices effortlessly.

Who is Rizz for?

Rizz is perfect for fashion-conscious young adults and millennials who love shopping online, follow the latest fashion trends, and value personalized shopping experiences. It also appeals to fashion enthusiasts looking for an AI tool to enhance their style, whether for everyday looks or special occasions.

How can Rizz help me replicate influencer fashion?

Rizz curates the latest trends from top influencers and suggests similar products that match their style. You can easily replicate the looks you admire by finding affordable and accessible alternatives that are available for purchase online.

What kind of recommendations does Rizz provide?

Rizz offers personalized recommendations for outfits, individual clothing pieces, and accessories. We suggest entire looks, smart pairings, and trendy items that complement your existing wardrobe, making it easier to shop for any occasion with confidence.

Can I use Rizz on different shopping platforms?

Absolutely! Rizz simplifies product discovery across multiple platforms, ensuring you find the best matches for your style preferences, no matter where you shop online.

How do I get started with Rizz?

Getting started is simple! Just download the Rizz app, log in with your phone number, and let the AI assistant guide you through personalized recommendations. It's like having a fashion expert in your pocket, available anytime and anywhere!
