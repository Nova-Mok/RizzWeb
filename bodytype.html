How to Identify Your Body Type

Knowing your body type is crucial for selecting clothing that highlights your best features and enhances your overall appearance. With the help of Flik’s AI-powered technology, identifying your body type becomes effortless, allowing you to make informed fashion choices.

1. Understanding Body Types

Body types are typically categorized to help guide fashion choices. The most common classifications include:

  • Hourglass: Balanced proportions with a defined waist.
  • Apple: Larger upper body with slimmer legs.
  • Pear: Wider hips and thighs with a smaller upper body.
  • Rectangle: Straight silhouette with minimal waist definition.
  • Inverted Triangle: Broader shoulders with narrower hips.

2. How to Determine Your Body Type

Follow these steps to identify your body type:

  • Measure Your Body: Take accurate measurements of your bust, waist, and hips.
  • Analyze Proportions: Compare your measurements to identify which parts of your body are more prominent.
  • Observe Your Silhouette: Stand in front of a mirror and assess your overall silhouette to see which category you fit into.

3. Dressing for Your Body Type

Once you’ve identified your body type, you can tailor your wardrobe to accentuate your strengths:

  • Hourglass: Highlight your waist with fitted clothing and use belts to emphasize your curves.
  • Apple: Opt for A-line dresses and tops that flow away from the midsection to create a balanced look.
  • Pear: Wear tops with detail and structure to draw attention upward, and choose bottoms that elongate your legs.
  • Rectangle: Create the illusion of curves with peplum tops and dresses that cinch at the waist.
  • Inverted Triangle: Balance your broad shoulders with fitted bottoms and avoid overly embellished tops.
